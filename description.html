Description

Go into an icy adventure with "Skadoosh," a thrilling mobile game where players slide down frosty slopes, dodging obstacles and outsmarting a hungry Yeti. With just a tap, navigate your sled left and right, weaving through challenges and collecting points along the way. Stay sharp and agile to avoid the Yeti's pursuit, keeping the excitement levels high. Challenge friends, beat your high score, and experience the rush of sledding in "Skadoosh"!.
